The Story

Built on Faith.
Proven by Work.

Taylored Media didn't start with a business plan or a safety net. It started with a layoff in April 2024, two years of grinding in the marketing world, and a choice — fold, or bet on yourself.

I chose to bet on myself. I picked up a camera, started showing up every single day, and built a media company from the ground up in Louisiana. No playbook. Just faith, consistency, and a commitment to doing the work.

Thirty-plus clients later, Taylored Media exists to do one thing: make your brand seen. Not just with pretty content — with content that actually moves your business forward.
