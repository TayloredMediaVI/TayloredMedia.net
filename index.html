In April 2024, I got laid off from a 3.5-year oilfield career. Instead of looking for the next job, I bet on myself and started building something of my own.

Taylored Media was built on a belief that God had something bigger planned. Today it's a full-service media agency serving local businesses across Louisiana — every piece of content crafted specifically for you, not copied from a template.

I built this business the same way I build content: show up, do the work, and don't stop until it's right.
